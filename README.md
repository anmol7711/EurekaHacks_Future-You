## ✨ What It Does

**Decade Clock** turns your daily habits into a **visual life trajectory**:

- Adjust your habits  
- Watch your **future timeline update instantly**  
- Compare your current path vs your **optimized self**

---

## ⚡ Core Features

### 📊 Real-Time Habit Engine
- 8 adjustable habit sliders  
- Instant recalculation of life outcomes  

### 🧠 Life Trajectory Score
- Unified score (0–100)  
- Combines:
  - Health  
  - Career  
  - Happiness  
  - Wealth  
  - Longevity  
  - Focus  
  - Energy  
  - Burnout risk  

### 📅 Dynamic Timeline
- Personalized life events at:
  - 25 · 30 · 35 · 40 · 50 · 60  
- Context-aware:
  - Milestones 🟢  
  - Warnings 🔴  
  - Neutral states 🟡  

### 👻 Ghost Mode
- Compare:
  - **You Now**
  - **Optimized You**
- Highlights the gap in key life metrics  

### ⚡ Auto-Optimize
- One-click habit upgrade  
- Animated transformation of your future  

### 📈 Future Vital Signs
- Visual dashboard of:
  - Health  
  - Career  
  - Happiness  
  - Burnout  
  - Focus  
  - Wealth  
  - Energy  
  - Longevity  

### 📤 Shareable Results
- Copy your score + stats instantly
